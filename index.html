About Me

After graduating Dresden High School as the salutatorian of her class, she pursued a successful career in electrical engineering before finding her true passion in programming. She is currently a computer science major with a concentration in digital hardware and embedded systems, and she is set to graduate in May of 2021. During her time at the University of Tennessee at Martin, Jennifer has contributed to multiple microcontroller-based projects, which can be viewed in the "Projects" section. Thanks to her interpersonal skills, she has been working successfully as a STEM Tutor for advanced mathematics and as a SI Leader for Computer Science.
